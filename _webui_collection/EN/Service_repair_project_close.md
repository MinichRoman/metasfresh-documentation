## Requirements
- [Process a returned product in need of repair](Service_repair_management_workflow).

## Steps
1. Open "Service/Repair Project" from the [menu](Menu).
1. [Use the filtering function](Filtering_function), to help you find the repair project you want to close and open the entry.
1. [Start the action](StartAction#actions-menu) "Close Project".
1. [metasfresh saves the progress automatically](Saveindicator).
